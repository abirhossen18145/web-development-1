    • Homemade Chicken Curry

    Here's a step-by-step process for making chicken curry:

    Ingredients :
    • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
    • 2 large onions, finely chopped
    • 3 tomatoes, chopped
    • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
    • 1/2 cup yogurt
    • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
      • (adjust according to your spice preference)
    • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
    • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh coriander leaves for garnis
    Instructions:
    1. Marination: In a bowl, mix the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes. You can refrigerate it for a more extended marination.
    2. Cooking the Chicken: Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
    3. Adding Spices: Once the onions are golden brown, add coriander powder and cumin powder. Sauté for a couple of minutes until the raw smell disappears.
    4. Adding Tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes to the pan and cook until they become soft and the oil starts separating from the masala.
    5. Cooking Marinated Chicken: Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook on medium heat until the chicken is well-sealed and browned.
    6. Simmering: Lower the heat, cover the pan, and let the chicken cook in its juices for about 15-20 minutes. Stir occasionally.
    7. Adjusting Spices: Add garam masala and adjust salt and chili powder according to your taste. Stir well.
    8. Cooking until Done: Continue cooking uncovered until the chicken is fully cooked, and the masala has thickened to your desired consistency.
    9. Garnishing: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
    10. Serving: Serve hot with steamed rice, naan, or your favorite bread.
